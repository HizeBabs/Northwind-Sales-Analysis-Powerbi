# Northwind-Sales-Analysis-Powerbi
This project presents a comprehensive sales analysis of the Northwind dataset using Power BI. The goal was to uncover key business insights, track performance, and support data-driven decision-making.

OBJECTIVES

Analyze total sales performance
Identify best and worst-selling products
Evaluate top customers by revenue
Track sales trends over time
Examine freight distribution across categories

 TOOLS USED
  Power BI
 Data Modeling
 DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)

 DASHBOARD FEATURES
KPI Cards: Total Sales (440.62K), Total Orders (270)
Best Selling Products visualization
Worst Selling Products analysis
Top Customers by Sales
Sales Trend Over Time
Freight Analysis by Category
Interactive filters (Year, Metrics)

KEY INSIGHTS
Certain products significantly outperform others in revenue generation
A small group of customers contributes a large percentage of total sales
Sales fluctuate over time, indicating seasonal or demand-based trends
Freight costs are evenly distributed across categories
